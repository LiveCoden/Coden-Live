  • What is the difference between local and organic SEO?

    There is often confusion about the difference between local and organic search engine optimization (SEO). Local SEO optimizes your website to be more successful in local searches. Organic SEO works to increase your website's ranking in results for popular search terms. For example, organic SEO helps you show up if someone searches "coffee shop. " Local SEO works for searches like "coffee shops near me. " You can use both types of SEO at the same time to improve your online presence.
